Love Beauty & Planet Tea Tree & Vetiver Body Wash
Prerna
Sep 4, 2014
ACNE,

Best Body Wash For Body Acne & Pimples

I normally suffer from BACK ACNE on and off because of my OILY ACNE PRONE SKIN and thats why this TEA TREE OIL BODY WASH caught my attention. The moment I saw this Love Beauty And Planet Tea Tree & Vetiver Body Wash I could not resist myself from buying it because of few reasons, one it had tea tree oil, which is amazing for acne prone skin and secondly because it was all natural, organic free from all kinds of chemicals like parabens, sulphate etc.

This is one body wash which is 100 % Organic and free from all types of chemicals be it sulpahte, paraben, or dye thats why its not only a good body cleanser for oily acne prone skin but for sensitive skin beauties also. Before I start with its review, lets check its details first.

Love Beauty And Planet Tea Tree & Vetiver Daily Detox Body Wash Details

Tea Tree Oil Body Wash For Back Acne comes in a beautiful round light green bottle with a flip cap which is neither too tight nor too loose. The body wash looks like a transparent gel and is of medium consistency. It didn’t lather much when I applied directly on my back but on using with loofah, it lathered amazingly. This body cleanser comes with a gentle fragrance of tea tree oil with a cooling touch. I believe its the combination of Tea & Vetiver which makes it like this.

EXPERIENCE & RESULT

I used this body wash last month because there could not be better time to test this body wash other than summer and rains together. During these months, our skin is most prone to acne and skin problems so I wanted to try this at that time. I used this body wash everyday for almost 2 weeks and the result was very clear after a week’s usage. My skin appeared noticeably clean and clear after few days. My skin redness and irritation which sometimes occur in rainy season also reduced soothing my skin making it softer and clearer. There were still few back acne but very less compared to what they were before. Along with getting rid of my body acne, this body wash did not irritate my skin in anyway.

Overall I would say I am in love with this Love, Beauty & Planet Body Wash because finally I have found a natural organic body wash with tea tree oil to help people with oily acne prone sensitive skin with back and body acne and nothing could be better than this. So if you also suffer from any of these problems, do give this a try. Here I would like to add one thing, this Love Beauty And Planet Body Cleanser is not so easily available in India, I got this on my trip to US, so might find it bit difficult to arrange it in India or could get shipped from US. This Tea Tree Oil and Vetiver is also available in form of Shampoo and face wash, so in case I get my hands on it, I will surely do its review.

PROS

  • 100 % Organic Natural Body Wash
  • Free from all chemicals
  • Pretty Convenient Packaging.
  • Fights Body & Back Acne in few uses.
  • Soothing & cooling fragrance.
  • Good for Oily Acne Prone Sensitive Skin.
  • Good for people with sensitive skin.

CONS

  • Not easily available in India
  • Not so travel friendly considering the big bottle.
